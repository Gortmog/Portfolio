Useful information:

This game is based on the general principles of the D&D system. This means that the outcome of every action is based on the dice roll. The chance to perform any action is defined by the roll of the 20-sided die (each side marked from 1 to 20 respectively). This is marked as 1d20 chance which means that ONE 20-sided die would be rolled.

In this game each time you try to hit a monster you will roll a die, then, depending on that roll there can be 3 possible outcomes:

- you hit;

- you miss;

- you crit (do additional damage, double in general)

Later in the game you will notice both hit and crit threshold of 1d20 as well as the dice roll for damage representation of your weapon. For example it can be declared as 2d4 which means that you are about to roll TWO 4-sided dice to calculate your output damage.

During the game you will make different choices: which path to choose, whether to hit or to use a potion, but first of all, you will have to choose who are you about to play: warrior, mage or rogue.

Now you are ready to start an epic journey to save the princess & to kill The Shadow! Good luck!

The story begins...

You are in the beginning of your journey. You heard stories about great evil on the south & now you search for any rumors that could lead you to its location. It's dusky already and you notice the inn along the road. You decide to stay here for the night.

While drinking your second pint of fine ale you see several soldiers enter the inn & approach the bartender. In couple of minutes you can hear those soldiers talking to each other about the news from the capital. The king is desperate - the princess has been kidnaped. And you hear the name: The Shadow... You recall this name and now you are sure that The Shadow is the source of the southern evil. Now your quest becomes as clear as it could be - you have to travel to the south, kill The Shadow and rescue the princess.

You rise up and approach to the soldiers.

- I will save the princess. Do you know where do I find The Shadow?

- What's your name, stranger? - one of the soldiers asks.

- All right, stranger, to find The Shadow you have to travel south all the way to the Dark Castle. There you will meet your destiny.

You thank soldiers and go to your room to sleep until the early morning. You have a strong feeling that you have found your predestination. Before you fall asleep you come up with the thoughts about the skills that will help you on your journey.
